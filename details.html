Sigiriya Fortress

$36 USD

Sigiriya is an ancient rock fortress featuring a royal palace and historic frescoes.

  1. Arrive by 06:30 AM to avoid midday heat.
  2. Walk through the symmetric water gardens.
  3. Ascend the final stairs to the summit.

Dalada Maliga

$15 USD

A sacred temple in Kandy housing the tooth relic of the Buddha.

  1. Wear modest attire covering shoulders and knees.
  2. Deposit footwear at the storage counter.
  3. Join the queue ascending to the relic room.

Mirissa Beach

Free

A quiet coastal beach town popular for surfing and whale watching.

  1. Enjoy a stroll along the sandy crescent.
  2. Rent a beach lounger from a local cafe.
  3. Walk to Coconut Tree Hill for sunset.
