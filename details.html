Sigiriya Fortress

Heritage Citadel

Entrance: $36 USD

Sigiriya is an ancient rock fortress located in the northern Matale District. King Kashyapa built his palace on top of this rock and decorated its sides with colorful frescoes.

Recommended route steps:

  1. Arrive at the ticket counter by 06:30 AM to avoid heat.
  2. Walk through the symmetric royal water gardens.
  3. Ascend the metal stairs past the celestial maidens frescoes.
  4. Pause at the Lion's Paw terrace before the final climb.
  5. Reach the summit ruins and enjoy the panoramic forest view.

Dalada Maliga

Sacred Shrine

Entrance: $15 USD

The Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic is a Buddhist temple in Kandy, Sri Lanka. It is located in the royal palace complex of the former Kingdom of Kandy, which houses the relic of the tooth of the Buddha.

Recommended route steps:

  1. Wear modest attire covering shoulders and knees (preferably white).
  2. Deposit footwear at the designated storage counter.
  3. Purchase entry token and cross the moat bridge.
  4. Join the queue ascending to the relic vault room.
  5. Visit the assembly hall to view historic paintings and murals.

Mirissa Beach

Seaside Coast

Entrance: Free

Mirissa is a small town on the south coast of Sri Lanka. It is popular for beach activities, whale watching excursions, and beautiful photo locations like the red-earth Coconut Tree Hill.

Recommended route steps:

  1. Enjoy a quiet morning stroll along the main sandy crescent.
  2. Rent a beach lounger from a wooden cafe.
  3. Walk to Coconut Tree Hill around 04:30 PM for sunset photos.
  4. Dine at beachside grills under candlelight.
